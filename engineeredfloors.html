Engineered /Veneered floors

Engineered floors or Veneered floors are made from two or more layers of wood with a top layer or Species layer ranging from 3-6mm thick.
They are available from 12mm up to 22mm thick, with widths ranging from 100mm up to 300mm.

Engineered flooring by Single Title Investments is available in both prefinished and unfinished. Our prefinished range is available in a variety of colors. Click here to view colors for our prefinished engineered flooring

Our unfinished range is available exclusively in Oak which can be customized to the preferred color. We work with only the best products to seal and oil your floors. We use the following products
  • Loba oils and varnishes
  • Monocoat oils and colors
 We can customize colors to meet individual needs.
